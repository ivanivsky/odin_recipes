Overnight Oats

Description

Overnight Oats are a quick means of having a nutritious breakfast. You can make several servings as part of your meal prep, and they stay good for a few days.

There are endless customizations you can make to this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Steelcut Oats
  • Chia seeds
  • Hemp seeds
  • Milk
  • Protein powder
  • Greek yogurt

Steps

  1. Get out all of your mason jars and funnel
  2. 1/2 cup of oats
  3. 3 tbp of hemp seeds
  4. 2 tbp of chia seeds
  5. 2 scoops of protein powder
  6. Cup of greek yogurt
  7. Fill the jar up with milk
