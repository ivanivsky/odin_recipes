Salmon and Mushrooms

Description

Salmon and Mushrooms is just a good all around dish

This is as healthy as it gets

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Mushrooms
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Avocado Oil

Steps

  1. Season the salmon and the mushrooms with salt, pepper, avocado oil and anything else
  2. Turn the air fryer on to 375
  3. Throw them in there for 8-10 minutes
