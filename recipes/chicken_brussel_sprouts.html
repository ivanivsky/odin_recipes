Chicken and Brussel Sprouts

Description

This is incredibly good.

The brussel sprouts make this the easiest meal ever

Ingredients

  • Uncooked chicken
  • Trader Joe's microwave brussel sprouts
  • Seasoning
  • Avocado oil

Steps

  1. Put the chicken on a baking pan and season and add oil
  2. Put the chicken in the over at 400 for 20 minutes
  3. Microwave the brussel sprouts
